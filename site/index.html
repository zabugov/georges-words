George’s Words

Hold a key. Say what you want to write. Let go — and polished, punctuated text appears right where you were typing.

Your voice never leaves your Mac. Transcription and cleanup run entirely on your computer — no cloud, no account, no data collection of any kind. That's the whole privacy policy.

Download for Mac

Requires a Mac with Apple Silicon (M1 or newer) and macOS 14 or later. Free.

Installing takes a minute

  1. Open the downloaded file and drag George's Words into Applications.
  2. Open it — a short welcome tour sets up everything, including the two one-time downloads it needs (about 1.6 GB).
  3. Hold the fn key anywhere you can type, and talk.
